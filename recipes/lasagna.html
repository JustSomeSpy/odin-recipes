Lasagna

description

A simple dish almost anyone can make. Trow it together, trow it into an oven and you are basically done.

Ingredients

  • 12 uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3 eggs
  • And even more!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees Celcius
  2. Bring a pot of water to boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 minutes
  3. Brown sausage and onions
  4. Figure the rest out for yourself!
